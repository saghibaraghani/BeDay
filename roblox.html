About this course

Roblox is a massively popular online gaming platform that allows users to create their own games and play games created by others. With over 150 million monthly active users, Roblox offers a vast and diverse range of games, from action and adventure to role-playing and simulation.
Lua, on the other hand, is the programming language used to create games and applications on Roblox. Lua is a lightweight and fast scripting language that is easy to learn and understand, making it an ideal language for beginners and advanced programmers alike.
Together, Roblox and Lua offer a unique and engaging platform for students to learn programming skills while creating their own games and experiences. With Roblox and Lua, students can explore their creativity, problem-solving skills, and teamwork abilities, all while having fun and learning valuable programming concepts.

What you'll learn

The exact content of a Roblox & Lua class will depend on the level of the class and the specific goals of the learners but generally we will learn:

• The basics of game development and design, including game mechanics, level design, and user interface design.

• How to create, customize, and publish games.

• The basics of the Lua programming language, including data types, variables, control structures, functions, and loops.

• How to design and create game levels that are challenging and engaging for players.
